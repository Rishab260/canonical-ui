Our Internal Platform

This platform is an internal project management and knowledge-sharing tool built to enhance visibility, documentation, and collaboration across Vaco Binary's technical and functional teams. It allows project owners and contributors to upload detailed information about each project, including its objective, scope, timeline, key milestones, and progress workflows. Each project entry is enriched with supporting artifacts such as documents, diagrams, screenshots, and external references to provide full project context. The platform also supports structured feedback at the end of each project, enabling teams to reflect, suggest improvements, and share outcomes with wider stakeholders. Designed for ease of use and scalability, this tool serves as a centralized hub to discover active initiatives, track ongoing work, and maintain a historical archive of completed projects across various departments and domains.
